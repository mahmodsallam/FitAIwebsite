Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected when using our apps.

This Privacy Policy applies to all identifier applications ("Apps") developed and published by us, including but not limited to Rock Identifier, Mushroom Identifier, Plant Identifier, and similar applications. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using our Apps, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our Apps are designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be clear about what we do NOT do:

  • No Personal Information Collection: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No Account Creation: Our Apps do not require user registration or authentication. There are no accounts to create or manage.
  • No Data Storage: We do not store any data you provide or generate while using the App on our servers.
  • No Location Tracking: We do not access or track your location.
  • No Contact Access: We do not access your contacts, calendar, or other personal information on your device.
  • No Analytics Collection: We do not use analytics tools to track your behavior within the App.

3. Images and Identification

When you use our Apps to identify objects (rocks, mushrooms, plants, etc.):

  • Images are processed to provide identification results.
  • Images are not stored on our servers after processing.
  • We do not retain, share, or use your images for any purpose other than providing the identification service.
  • All processing is done to deliver the service you requested and nothing more.
